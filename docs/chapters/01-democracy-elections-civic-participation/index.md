# Democracy, Elections, and Civic Participation

## Summary

This chapter introduces the foundational concepts of democracy and civic participation that underpin all volunteer campaign work. You will learn about the different types of elections — primary, general, local, state, and federal — and how election cycles shape the timing and urgency of volunteer efforts. The chapter also covers voting districts, precincts, voter turnout, and how competitive races create opportunities for volunteers to make a meaningful impact. After completing this chapter, you will understand the broader electoral landscape in which canvassing and voter outreach take place.
