# Becoming a Volunteer

## Summary

This chapter covers everything you need to know about becoming a campaign volunteer, from finding opportunities and understanding roles to training, onboarding, and sustaining your motivation over time. You will learn about volunteer coordinators, etiquette, expectations, time commitment, and what keeps volunteers engaged and coming back. After completing this chapter, you will be ready to sign up for your first volunteer shift with confidence and realistic expectations.
