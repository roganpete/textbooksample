# Chapters

This textbook is organized into 13 chapters covering 200 concepts.

## Chapter Overview

1. [Democracy, Elections, and Civic Participation](01-democracy-elections-civic-participation/index.md) - Introduces the foundations of democratic participation, election types and cycles, voting districts, and how volunteer efforts connect to the broader electoral landscape.
2. [How Political Campaigns Work](02-how-political-campaigns-work/index.md) - Explores campaign structure, strategy, field operations, voter data systems, and grassroots organizing.
3. [Canvassing Fundamentals and Tools](03-canvassing-fundamentals-and-tools/index.md) - Covers the basics of canvassing as a voter contact method, including turf management, walk lists, canvassing apps, and data recording.
4. [Scripts, Messaging, and the Canvassing Conversation](04-scripts-messaging-canvassing-conversation/index.md) - Teaches how canvassing scripts are structured, from introduction and message delivery through the ask and closing.
5. [Becoming a Volunteer](05-becoming-a-volunteer/index.md) - Covers finding volunteer opportunities, understanding roles and expectations, training, onboarding, and sustaining motivation.
6. [Your First Volunteer Shift](06-your-first-volunteer-shift/index.md) - Walks through every aspect of a first canvassing shift, from preparation and check-in through debriefing and building confidence.
7. [Door Knocking Skills and Safety](07-door-knocking-skills-and-safety/index.md) - Covers the practical mechanics of door-to-door canvassing, including approach techniques, safety, and adapting to different environments.
8. [Face-to-Face Conversation Skills](08-face-to-face-conversation-skills/index.md) - Develops interpersonal communication skills for voter interactions, including rapport building, active listening, and navigating disagreement.
9. [Phone Banking and Text Banking](09-phone-banking-and-text-banking/index.md) - Covers both phone and text voter outreach, including technology, scripts, compliance, metrics, and scaling operations.
10. [Persuasive Conversations with Voters](10-persuasive-conversations-with-voters/index.md) - Teaches persuasion techniques for engaging undecided voters, including issue framing, storytelling, and ethical considerations.
11. [Get Out the Vote Operations](11-get-out-the-vote-operations/index.md) - Covers GOTV strategy, timeline, Election Day logistics, and the shift from persuasion to mobilization.
12. [Deep Canvassing](12-deep-canvassing/index.md) - Explores the deep canvassing method, its research basis, narrative exchange technique, and empathy-based conversations.
13. [Measuring Effectiveness and Next Steps](13-measuring-effectiveness-and-next-steps/index.md) - Covers self-assessment, data analysis, multi-method volunteering, and applied capstone projects.

## How to Use This Textbook

Chapters are designed to be read in order, as each one builds on concepts from previous chapters. Early chapters establish the civic and campaign context, middle chapters develop practical volunteer skills across multiple outreach methods, and later chapters cover advanced techniques and strategic planning. If you are already an experienced volunteer, you may skip to specific chapters, but be sure to review the prerequisites listed at the top of each chapter.
