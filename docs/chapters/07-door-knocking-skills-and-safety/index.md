# Door Knocking Skills and Safety

## Summary

This chapter covers the practical mechanics of door-to-door canvassing, from approaching a door and knocking to identifying voters and handling no-answer situations. You will learn about personal safety, the buddy system, dealing with dogs and obstacles, and adapting your approach for different environments including apartments, gated communities, and rural areas. The chapter also covers physical considerations like walking pace, stamina, and weather challenges. After completing this chapter, you will be equipped to handle any door knocking scenario safely and effectively.
