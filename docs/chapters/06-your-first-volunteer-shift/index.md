# Your First Volunteer Shift

## Summary

This chapter walks you through every aspect of your first volunteer canvassing shift, from preparation and logistics to debriefing and personal growth. You will learn what to bring, what to wear, how check-in and briefing work, and how to handle post-shift data reporting. The chapter also addresses common anxieties, managing expectations, and expanding your comfort zone. After completing this chapter, you will feel prepared and confident walking into your first shift.
