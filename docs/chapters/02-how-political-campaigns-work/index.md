# How Political Campaigns Work

## Summary

This chapter explores how political campaigns are organized and run, from high-level strategy and timelines to the ground-level field operations that mobilize volunteers. You will learn about campaign staff roles, voter files, voter targeting, and how campaigns identify their voter universe and set contact rate goals. The chapter also covers grassroots organizing and how earned media differs from paid media. After completing this chapter, you will understand the campaign infrastructure that supports all volunteer outreach activities.
