# Persuasive Conversations with Voters

## Summary

This chapter teaches the art and science of persuading undecided and persuadable voters through conversation. You will learn about issue framing, values-based messaging, storytelling, emotional and logical appeals, and advanced techniques like motivational interviewing and inoculation messaging. The chapter also addresses common pitfalls, the importance of respecting voter autonomy, and how to find common ground even when you disagree. After completing this chapter, you will have a toolkit of ethical persuasion techniques to use in voter conversations.
